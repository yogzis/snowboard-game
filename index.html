How to Play

Controls

⬅️ ➡️ | Steer (curve intensity increases with hold)
⬆️ | Lean forward + steer = controlled turn
⬇️ | Lean back = speed up; with steer = brake
␣ Space | Tap = short jump; hold = charge long jump (green bar)
Esc | Pause / Resume

Speed & control

  • Up + steer caps speed at 60% for precise control.
  • Down + steer brakes smoothly to 1 km/h.
  • Down alone increases speed.
  • Steering only (no Up/Down) caps at 75% with a slide feel.
  • Hold a turn too long (~1.5 s) and you spin out.

Jumping

Tap Space for a short jump; hold Space to charge a higher long jump (green bar). Long jump is capped above the old high jump. Ramps launch you into the air; tap Space again in the air after a ramp for an extra boost to reach elevated boxes.

Obstacles

Trees and rocks: 20 damage and 50% speed loss on hit. Ramps: hit them to get launched for air time.

Mystery boxes

Jump on boxes for rewards. Standard boxes are floating; elevated boxes (often after ramps) need a ramp launch plus a Space tap in the air to reach. Hitting a box from the side removes it without a reward.

Rewards

Speed boost, HP refill, extra life or full heal, and a temporary shield (invincibility).

Dynamite (trap)

Some boxes give dynamite: a chain timer (~5 s), then 50 damage. To disarm, jump twice in a row without steering (no Left/Right input) to detach before it explodes.

Exit to menu? Your current progress will be lost.
